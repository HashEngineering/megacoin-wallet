Have your Megacoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Megacoin Wallet is the first mobile Megacoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Megacoin amount in DGC and mDGC.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Megacoin via NFC, QR-codes or Megacoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Megacoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Megacoin balance.
